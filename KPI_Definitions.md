# Key Performance Indicator Definitions  
## FinTime – Customer Financial Behavior & Health Intelligence System  
### Version: 1.0  

---

| KPI Name | Definition | Purpose | Owner |
|----------|------------|----------|--------|
| Total Customers | Count of unique customers | Measures customer base size | Leadership |
| Total Transactions | Total number of recorded financial transactions | Measures activity volume | Analytics |
| Total Transaction Value | SUM(amount) | Indicates revenue contribution | Finance |
| Average Monthly Spend | Total Spend / Active Months | Evaluates customer value contribution | BI Team |
| Active Customers | Customers with ≥1 transaction in last 90 days | Customer engagement metric | Strategy |
| Churn Rate | % customers inactive for >90 days | Retention risk indicator | CX Team |
| Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) | Expected financial contribution per customer | Prioritization & targeting | Business |
| Financial Health Score | Composite metric based on recency, frequency, monetary patterns | Risk exposure & early warning system | Risk Team |
| High-Risk Customers | Count of customers below risk threshold | Proactive management control | Risk |
